### Description
```
DonNote — the new standard in personal finance.
Manage every transaction and asset in one clean view.

■ Quick Transaction Logging
Record expenses, income, and transfers in a single screen. Automatic thousands separator, native number keyboard, and a one-line input for a frictionless feel.

■ Unified Asset Management
Bank accounts, cash, debit cards, credit cards, loans, and stocks — track every asset and your total net worth in one place.

■ Automatic Balance Updates
Asset balances refresh the moment you record a transaction. Expenses subtract, income adds, transfers adjust both sides simultaneously. No manual math.

■ Credit Card Installments
Auto-generate 2 to 24-month installment transactions with accurate equal-principal interest calculation based on remaining balance. Your monthly billing flow is reflected exactly.

■ Loan Principal Repayment
Use a transfer to send money from your bank to a loan — repayments are applied instantly and your debt balance decreases automatically.

■ Monthly Statistics
Donut charts and category breakdowns reveal exactly where your money goes. Compare income, expenses, and net profit month over month.

■ iCloud Sync
Transactions, assets, and categories sync automatically across iPhone and iPad. No sign-up or login required. Your data is stored only in your own iCloud account.

■ Face ID / Touch ID Lock
Protect sensitive financial information with biometric authentication.

■ Custom Categories
Build your own categories with emojis and colors, and reorder them anytime.

■ CSV & JSON Export
Export transactions to Excel-compatible CSV, or back up all data as JSON.

■ Foreign Currency Support
Register assets in USD, JPY, and other currencies with automatic exchange rate conversion.
```

## 📝 App Review Information → Notes

```
SIGN-IN: Not required. The app opens directly with no account or login.

SUBSCRIPTIONS (Guideline 3.1.2(c)):
The paywall (Settings → "Premium Subscription") shows: title ("DonNote Premium"),
subscription length (1 month / 1 year), price (from StoreKit), and functional links
to the Terms of Use (Apple Standard EULA) and Privacy Policy. The App Description
also contains the EULA and Privacy Policy links. The same links are in Settings → Info.

IN-APP PURCHASE (Guideline 2.1(b)):
Paid Applications Agreement is active. Both subscriptions
(com.yoonLab.DonNote.premium.monthly / .yearly) are attached to this version
in "Ready to Submit" state and load correctly in the sandbox.

APP TRACKING TRANSPARENCY (Guideline 2.1):
The ATT permission request appears automatically on first launch (after ~0.8s,
once the UI renders) before AdMob initializes. NSUserTrackingUsageDescription
is set in Info.plist.

ADS: Free users see Google AdMob native ads (transactions, assets, inbox,
statistics screens). Subscribers see no ads. Settings screen is ad-free.

DATA: All data is stored on-device and in the user's own iCloud (CloudKit).
No developer backend. Exchange rate / stock price APIs receive no user data.

HOW TO TEST:
1. Launch — a default cash asset is auto-created; ATT dialog appears.
2. Tap "+" to add a transaction (category, amount, payment method).
3. Open a credit card asset to add installment transactions.
4. Statistics tab shows the monthly donut chart.
5. Settings → "Premium Subscription" opens the paywall.

Thank you for the review!
```
